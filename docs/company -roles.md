# Company roles

- AUTHORITY — Short: Legal authority. Official body with legal power: permits, inspections, enforcement, fines, seizures, rulings (port authority, customs, court, navy office).
- BANK — Short: Banking institution. Runs accounts and payments, holds deposits, issues loans and guarantees, handles letters of credit and secure transfers.
- BROKER — Short: Deal broker. Connects parties and negotiates deals for a commission; typically arranges terms but does not physically move or store goods.
- COMMS_PROVIDER — Short: Comms provider. Provides communications services: relays, message routing, data courier dispatch, secure channels, comms infrastructure access.
- ENERGY_UTILITY — Short: Power utility. Supplies and manages power/fuel distribution for stations and settlements: generation, grid service, contracts for steady supply.
- FINANCIER — Short: Venture financier. Provides investment and high-risk financing: funding ventures, underwriting projects, managing assets for returns rather than basic banking.
- HOSPITALITY — Short: Passenger hospitality. Provides passenger-facing services: lodging, berths, catering, lounges, bookings, and general comfort services.
- INDUSTRIAL_SUPPLIER — Short: Industrial supplier. Supplies industrial inputs: spare parts, components, tools, materials, consumables, and standardized replacement stock.
- LOGISTICS_HUB — Short: Logistics hub. Handles warehousing and cargo operations: storage, consolidation, loading/unloading, scheduling, bonded/controlled storage, handoff coordination.
- MANUFACTURER — Short: Manufacturer. Produces goods through fabrication/assembly: factories, workshops, production lines, and contract manufacturing.
- MEDIA_NETWORK — Short: Media network. Produces and distributes information and entertainment: news, advertising, broadcasts, propaganda, and content syndication.
- MEDICAL_PROVIDER — Short: Medical provider. Delivers medical services and supplies: clinics/medbays, treatment, pharmaceuticals, emergency care, med-evac coordination.
- MINING_COMPANY — Short: Mining operator. Extracts raw resources: ore, volatiles, ice, gas harvesting; primary collection before refinement/processing.
- MULTI_SERVICE_PROVIDER — Short: Multi-service operator. General-purpose company that offers multiple services under one roof (one-stop operator), used when the exact specialty isn’t worth tracking.
- REFINERY — Short: Refinery. Processes raw inputs into usable products: fuels, purified chemicals, refined metals/alloys, and industrial-grade materials.
- RESEARCH_LAB — Short: Research lab. Performs R&D and testing: prototypes, experiments, trials, validation, and controlled development of new tech or processes.
- SECURITY_CONTRACTOR — Short: Security contractor. Provides security services: escort, protection, investigation, risk assessment, armed response where legal.
- SHIPYARD — Short: Shipyard. Builds and overhauls ships: construction, major repairs, refits, drydock services, and certified heavy maintenance.
- TECH_VENDOR — Short: Technology vendor. Sells/installs/supports technology systems: software, electronics, sensors, avionics, networks, diagnostics, and maintenance contracts.
- TRADER — Short: Trade operator. Trades goods for profit: sourcing, buying low/selling high, managing inventory and market risk as a core business.
